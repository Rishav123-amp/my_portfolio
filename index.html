Classified Profile

Rishabh Kumar is a game developer obsessed with atmosphere — the kind of dread, awe, or wonder that lives in lighting, sound design, and the negative space between objects.

Working across Unity, C#, and Blender, his focus is immersive horror and cinematic world-building: environments that feel inhabited, lived-in, and just slightly wrong in the right places.

Every project starts the same way — not with mechanics, but with a feeling he wants the player to carry out of the room.
